Welcome to the WPF Handler framework!

Thank you for deciding to use the WPF Handler at your project!

The WPF Handler is a powerful framework that allow you to simplify your development and cover a lot of complex tasks to save your time and money. The product licensed by the Apache 2.0 agreement, that make it available for including into commercial products without royalty.
